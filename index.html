Why do we need the discovery phase

    Very probably, No

  • Can we decrease the expected development time?

    It's possible, at least we will have some realistic development plan

  • Can we decrease the scope of the project?

    Yes and no. See the image in the right

  • Can we increase the level of client's satisfaction?

    Yes

Obviously, we have to have Sprint 0 before the estimate, no matter who pays for it.

 

The main reason that we must to have not only successfull project done in time and inside the budget. We must have sutisfied client that means he/she get the maximum value inside the spent budget.
